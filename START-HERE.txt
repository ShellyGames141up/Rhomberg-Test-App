WHAT TO TEST
1. Sign in as Sales and open the assigned Cape Process Demo RFQ.
2. Mark the quotation sent, move it to customer acceptance, confirm acceptance,
   then convert it. The order number is created automatically.
3. Sign in as Planning, open the new order, add the test job/PO numbers and
   submit it to Expediting.
4. Sign in as Expeditor, start the order and hand it to Dispatch.
5. Sign in as Dispatch, follow its collection path and complete the order.
6. Sign in as the Cape customer (shown on the sign-in screen) to review the
   RFQ, separate order timeline and notifications.
7. Try the notification read state and light/dark theme.

IMPORTANT
Accounts, RFQs, orders, notifications and updates persist in this browser only.
They remain after the site is closed and reopened, but do not synchronise to
another phone or browser. Use fabricated test information only. The production
version needs secure authentication, a shared database, server transactions,
notification delivery and encrypted Purchase Order storage.
