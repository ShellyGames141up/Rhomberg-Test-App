WHAT TO TEST
1. Sign in as the Cape customer and submit an RFQ to Ericu Vercuiel using
   fabricated information. Confirm the permanent reference and routing message.
2. Sign in as Sales. Find the RFQ in New RFQs, open it and select Start Review.
3. Use the Sales inbox groups, search and priority filters. Confirm RFQ age,
   company/customer information, emergency priority and last activity are clear.
4. Open the assigned Cape Process Demo RFQ and select Mark as Quoted. Enter a
   fabricated quotation number/date, optionally record its expiry and Outlook
   email confirmation, and use separate internal and customer-facing notes.
5. Sign in as the Cape customer, open that RFQ in Order Tracking and select
   I received the quotation. Confirm this only records receipt and does not
   create an order, confirm payment or accept pricing.
6. Sign back in as Sales. Confirm the acknowledgement notification, record the
   separate external customer-order acceptance evidence and select Accept Order.
   Try a fabricated PO acceptance with a test PO number, date, internal note and
   verification checkbox. The RFQ and one permanent order number are linked
   automatically and the order is routed to Planning. For a quicker demo, open
   the pre-seeded RQ-TEST-0006, which is already awaiting customer acceptance.
7. Sign in as Planning. The desktop workspace has fabricated orders in Awaiting,
   In Progress and Planned stages. Search/filter/sort the queue, open an Awaiting
   order and select Start Planning. Open the In Progress order, enter a fabricated
   internal job number, customer PO (or an authorised test exception), owner,
   submission date, location and schedule, then Save planning details. Open the
   Planned order and select Submit to Expediting. Confirm the customer sees only
   the safe hand-off update while Expediting receives its queue notification.
8. Sign in as Expeditor. Use the New, In Progress, Due Soon, On Hold and
   Awaiting Dispatch views, then search by customer, rep, RFQ, order, job or PO.
   Open a newly submitted order and select Start Expediting. Add configured
   progress updates with a customer message and a separate fabricated internal
   note. Change the estimate, record a delay, place it on hold and resume it.
   Complete the required hand-off steps, then Submit to Dispatch. Confirm the
   handed-off order stays visible but read-only in Awaiting Dispatch.
9. Sign in as Dispatch, follow its collection path and complete the order.
10. Sign in as the Cape customer (shown on the sign-in screen) to review the
   RFQ, separate order timeline and notifications.
11. Try the notification read state and light/dark theme.
12. Sign in as Buyer and confirm that no operational queue or update action is
   exposed while the procurement workflow remains inactive.
13. Sign in as Manager and Administrator to confirm their wider oversight
   queues. Workflow actions remain constrained by the current stage and the
   central permission catalogue.

EXPECTED QUEUE ISOLATION
- Customer: only the authorised company.
- Sales inbox: only RFQs assigned to that representative; assigned orders remain
  visible from the representative account summary.
- Planning: awaiting planning, planning in progress, planned, or a Planning hold.
- Expeditor: submitted to expediting, expediting in progress, an Expediting hold,
  or read-only awaiting-Dispatch hand-off awareness.
- Dispatch: awaiting dispatch through delivery/collection, or a Dispatch hold.
- Buyer: no operational records until its workflow is approved.
- Manager/Administrator: wider authorised operational oversight.

IMPORTANT
Accounts, RFQs, orders, notifications and updates persist in this browser only.
They remain after the site is closed and reopened, but do not synchronise to
another phone or browser. Use fabricated test information only. The production
version needs secure authentication, a shared database, server transactions,
notification delivery and encrypted Purchase Order storage.

SAFETY
Accept Order records only evidence already received through the approved external
sales process. Do not enter real prices, payment-card details, bank credentials,
PINs or passwords. Supporting-file bytes are not retained in this mock preview.
Planning notes, job numbers, schedules, locations and document references are
internal-only. Expediting internal notes, delay/reference context and exception
evidence are also internal-only. Use fabricated values in the public test preview.
